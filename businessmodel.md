# AskAndrew.io - Business Model & Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Identity

* **Name:** AskAndrew.io
* **Core Value Proposition:** Fast-tracking ideas from raw concepts into functional, clicking web prototypes and custom AI environments using a hybrid of traditional programming and advanced AI coding tools.
* **The Big Picture:** We bridge the expert gap. We take founders, creators, and professionals from "I have an idea" to a working, interactive piece of web software or a custom-built AI workspace in a single afternoon.

## 2. Target Customer

Our ideal clients are those who:

* **Have brilliant web app or tool concepts but lack the coding background to build them.** (e.g., non-technical founders, bootstrappers, indie hackers).
* **Are wasting hours trying to build with AI code assistants on their own** and getting caught in endless, frustrating bug loops or broken code files.
* **Need a highly tailored AI workspace** that actually understands their specific industry data, templates, and operational rules so they can stop prompting from scratch every day.
* **Specific Roles:** Independent Creators, Pre-Seed Founders, Solo-preneurs, and forward-thinking Professionals (like Real Estate Agents or Consultants).

## 3. Brand Voice & Copy Style

* **Tone:** Empathetic, witty, conversational, direct, and slightly humorous.
* **Vibe:** We acknowledge the pain and frustration of bad tech ("There has to be an easier way") and provide a clear, confident, and "chill" path to resolution ("No problem!").
* **Imagery:** Minimalist, single-panel comic style (inspired by "The Far Side" by Gary Larson). Employs humorous, simple flat-colored illustrations of frustrated vs. relieved/happy faces and messy vs. clean desks.
* **Key Phrasing:**
* "Bring Your Ideas to Life."
* "There has to be an easier way."
* "Stop talking about your app idea. Let’s build it this afternoon."
* "Zero to MVP at the speed of thought."



## 4. Core Capabilities (The Engine)

Instead of generalized web development or complex server hosting, everything we build utilizes fast, highly effective development environments:

* **AI-Accelerated Rapid Prototyping:** Combining traditional programming foundations with state-of-the-art AI development environments to spin up gorgeous, high-contrast, modern UI wrappers around software ideas in record time.
* **Advanced AI Logic (Google AI Studio):** Designing deep backend prompt logic, structured system instructions, and robust JSON inputs/outputs natively within Google AI Studio.
* **Bespoke AI Workspaces:** Building highly contextual, template-loaded internal tools and tailored dashboard settings to automate repetitive writing, analysis, or administrative tasks.

## 5. The Booking Menu (The "Kolander" Offer Architecture)

### 🔴 Live Web App Prototyping

* **Price:** Starting from $450
* **The Pitch:** Bring your concept and API keys. We'll build your clicking MVP live on screen share using advanced AI tools and traditional code before your coffee gets cold.
* **What you get:** The code files ready for users/investors, plus a 14-day bug-support window.
* **Consultant Note:** This is your flagship service. While $450 sounds like a lot for 90 minutes, a founder would easily spend $2,000+ to get a raw prototype from a traditional agency. Because you include the 14-day basic support window to fix minor bugs, this represents massive value for them and great margins for you.

### 🟡 Custom AI Workspace Setup

* **Price:** Starting from $225
* **The Pitch:** Stop pasting the same prompts every day. We’ll build a private, tailored AI environment live on a call to automate your repetitive writing and daily data tasks.
* **What you get:** A fully configured, private workspace pre-loaded with your operational rules.
* **Consultant Note:** Traditional consulting commands $150-$250/hr. These 45-minute slots sit comfortably in that sweet spot while promising a highly specific asset deliverable at the end.

### 🔵 App Updates & Debugging

* **Price:** $99
* **The Pitch:** *Exclusively for returning clients.* Workflows evolve and bugs happen. Book this rapid live sprint to push new features, tweak system instructions, or fix a recent break.
* **What you get:** Live code updates and seamless deployment.
* **Consultant Note:** Offering a discounted rate exclusively for return clients builds massive loyalty. It turns a one-time MVP build into a recurring, predictable stream of income as their product grows.

### 🟢 Technical Feasibility & Roadmap

* **Price:** $65
* **The Pitch:** Have a raw app or tool idea? Let’s vet it before you waste savings on an agency. We'll map out your concept, pick your tech stack, and design a lean build strategy.
* **What you get:** A concrete technical roadmap and zero corporate fluff.
* **Consultant Note:** At $65, this acts as a low-friction entry point that filters out time-wasters, while paying you a solid rate for 20 minutes of pure technical diagnostic work.

## 6. Value Delivery & Guardrails

* **Tangible Assets:** Clients never leave a paid call with just "advice"—they leave with an actual, functioning workspace, custom tool setup, or code file they can interact with immediately.
* **The Validation Guardrail:** We explicitly position our builds as *proofs-of-concept and MVPs* designed to test market demand, not infinitely scalable enterprise software.
* **Client Autonomy:** By coding live via screen share and breaking down the logic simply, we hand over the keys so the client feels empowered to continue tweaking their new tool long after the call ends.
* **Refund Policy:** Due to the bespoke nature of my services, all sales are final. Refunds cannot be offered once the engagement has started.
